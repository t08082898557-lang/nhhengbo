Cookie Policy

The website may use essential browser storage to remember the products you add to the B2B Quote List and the language selected for the current page. The Quote List does not contain prices and is not a payment cart.

Inquiry measurement

When the secure inquiry backend is connected, the service may record the inquiry source page, language and UTM parameters so the sales team can follow up accurately. No unnecessary sensitive information should be submitted through the form.

Managing storage

You can clear local browser storage at any time. Clearing it removes the locally saved Quote List from that browser.

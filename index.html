Buyer FAQ

Questions before you request a quotation.

Can I request samples before a bulk order?

Yes. Submit the model, sample quantity, receiving country and specification requirements. Sample cost and international shipping will be confirmed before payment.

Can I quote several models together?

Yes. Add products to the Quote List, set the expected quantity and version for each model, then submit one multi-product inquiry.

Do you support private-label packaging?

Logo, housing color, color box, instruction sheet and carton marks can be discussed according to model and confirmed project requirements.

Is this a ready-stock retail store?

No. HENGBO is a B2B inquiry-led supplier. Product configuration, quantity, packaging and production details are confirmed before quotation and production.

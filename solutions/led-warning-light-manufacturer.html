Buyer Questions

Common questions before sourcing

Can HENGBO support OEM warning light orders?

Yes. Logo, color box, instruction sheet and export packing can be discussed for suitable order quantities.

Which models are suitable for a first inquiry?

HB-362, HB-363C, HB-329, HB-331 and HB-361 are good starting models for roadside safety and emergency warning ranges.

Can I request samples?

Yes. Send target models, quantity, destination market and packaging needs to confirm sample options.

Buyer Guide

How to choose LED warning lights for your market.

Different buyers do not need the same warning light. Vehicle emergency kits, rechargeable safety programs, towing rescue products and road-work visibility projects each need a different model mix. This guide helps overseas buyers decide before requesting samples or OEM packaging.

Ask HENGBO for model adviceView warning light products

Step 1

Choose by use case first.

  • Roadside emergency kits: compact battery flares and triangle warning lights
  • Reusable fleet programs: rechargeable warning lights and magnetic beacons
  • Towing or rescue vehicles: high-visibility magnetic beacons and red-blue emergency lights
  • Retail packs: easy battery replacement, clear packaging and simple instructions

Step 2

Battery vs rechargeable.

Battery typeBest for emergency kits, backup stock, retail shelves and markets that prefer low first cost.
Rechargeable typeBest for reusable safety products, professional buyers, fleet programs and higher perceived value.
Mixed orderMany importers quote both types to test the market before a larger OEM order.

Buyer FAQ

Common selection questions

Should I choose battery or rechargeable LED warning lights?

Battery warning lights are easy for emergency kits and backup stock. Rechargeable warning lights are better for reusable programs, fleet buyers and repeat-use safety products.

Which HENGBO models are best for roadside emergency kits?

HB-362, HB-363, HB-363C, HB-330 and HB-331 are strong starting models for roadside emergency kits and OEM warning light packs.

What should overseas buyers send for a warning light quotation?

Send model, target quantity, battery or rechargeable preference, target market, packaging requirements, logo needs and expected delivery time.
